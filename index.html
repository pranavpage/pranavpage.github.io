Hi!

I'm Pranav — a curious engineer who loves building things that solve real problems.

I currently work as a Data Scientist at Carnot Technologies, where I develop analytics products using telemetry from thousands of tractors — combining geospatial intelligence, machine learning, and LLM-based tools to support both farmers and OEMs. Previously, I was a Modeling Systems Engineer at Qualcomm, working on receiver algorithms that power Wi-Fi chips in mobile and networking devices.

I graduated from IIT Bombay in 2023 with a dual degree (B.Tech + M.Tech) in Electrical Engineering, specializing in communications and signal processing, along with a minor in Computer Science. I'm especially interested in IoT, algorithms, ML systems, and tools that bridge data and decision-making.
